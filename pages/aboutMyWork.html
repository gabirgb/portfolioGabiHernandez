About my work

Outdoor painting

Painting outdoors is a deeply sensory experience. The environment becomes part of the process: light shifts, wind intervenes, sounds accompany. Each landscape offers a unique atmosphere that interacts with the work. Being outside the studio invites spontaneity, direct observation, and contact with the living world. It’s a way to connect with the present moment, to be affected by what’s happening, and to translate it into color and form.

Mixed media artworks

Mixed media artworks offer a creative freedom that transcends the boundaries of traditional materials. By combining elements such as paint, collage, objects, textures, and surfaces, a space for exploration opens where the visual and the tactile engage in dialogue. This practice invites experimentation, breaking structures, and building layers of meaning. Each piece becomes a unique territory where the intuitive and the conceptual coexist. Mixed media allows for new ways of storytelling, representing the symbolic, and connecting with emotion through materiality and the unexpected.

My language

Painting is a language that transcends words. Through color, form, and composition, it communicates emotions, ideas, and symbols that invite multiple interpretations. Symbolism in painting creates bridges between the visible and the invisible, revealing deep and personal meanings within each work.
